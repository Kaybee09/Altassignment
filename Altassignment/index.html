Biography

My name is kb, and I have always been driven by a deep curiosity for how things work, especially in the realm of technology. Growing up, I was the type of person who loved taking apart gadgets, whether it was an old mobile phone, a radio, or even a computer. My fascination with the inner workings of machines led me to pursue a formal education in computer science. I earned a degree in Business management from Lithuania and a degree in computer science, where I gained more solid foundation on how business work and the important of innovation and technnology.

During my time at university, I worked on several projects that honed my skills in various areas of engineering. Whether it was developing apps, automating processes, or collaborating with teams on hardware-software integration projects, I quickly realized that my passion for technology extended beyond just theory—it was the ability to use this knowledge to solve real-world problems that truly inspired me. After graduation, I worked with my previous company, where I contributed to CRM software project deepening my expertise in areas like project management, product design and collaborative problem-solving.

When I am not immersed in the world of code and circuits, I enjoy playing video games, wating movies, and playing working out. These activities enhance problem-solving skills, boost creativity, improve focus and productivity, encourage collaboration and relaxation and stress relief

purpose of joining AltSchool

I chose to join AltSchool because it offers an immersive learning experience that stands out from traditional education models. AltSchool’s emphasis on project-based learning, where students tackle real-world challenges, resonates with my belief in learning by doing. This approach lets me put theory into practice, helping me grow faster as an engineer.

ALTSCHOOL offers a hands-on learning experience focused on real-world applications, mentorship from industry professionals, and opportunities to work on innovative projects. ThE environment is ideal for me as i'm looking to accelerate my career in technology, stay ahead of industry trends, and develop both technical expertise and soft skills like problem-solving and collaboration. Joining ALTSCHOOL is a strategic step toward achieving my goals in the tech field.

Additionally, the flexibility of the School of Engineering Program at AltSchool allows for personalization, enabling me to concentrate on areas I’m passionate about while exploring emerging technologies. This adaptability, coupled with a strong network of industry professionals, will help me grow more rapidly and better prepare for the challenges of the modern tech landscape.

I want to build on my existing knowledge, but I am particularly excited about exploring new areas, such as artificial intelligence, blockchain technology, and cloud infrastructure. By working on these projects, I hope to enhance my problem-solving skills, gain experience in managing complex engineering processes, and learn how to deliver scalable and efficient solutions in a collaborative environment.

I am also looking to expand my professional network through the program, connecting with both my peers and mentors. Building these relationships will not only help me learn from others but also open doors to future career opportunities. I aspire to one day contribute to impactful projects in areas such as fintech, healthcare technology, or even education, and I believe that AltSchool will provide me with the tools and connections to make that dream a reality.

Finally, my personal mission is to use technology to improve lives, whether by increasing efficiency in systems or by developing solutions that address critical societal challenges. The School of Engineering Program at AltSchool offers a platform where I can align my technical pursuits with my desire to make a difference, empowering me to grow both personally and professionally.
